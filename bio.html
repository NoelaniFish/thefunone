Bio: Who Did This

Hello! My name is Noelani (Nani) Fishman. I am an illustrator and designer from Knoxville, Tennessee with a love for the whimsical and the absurd. I graduated from Pratt Institute with a Bachelors in Communications Design, focusing in Illustration and a minor in Philosophy. Currently, I am studying in Design and Technology at Parsons School of Design. Check out my instagram @noelanifishman or my other website noelanifishman.com for more work!
